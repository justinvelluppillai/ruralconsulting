Ian Brown

  •  Principal Advisor

Ian has a wealth of experience in environmental and resource policy and management developed over his 50 year career working in catchment boards, regional councils and as a consultant. Ian is an expert in resource management, regional policy implementation, soil conservation, sustainable land use, farm environmental planning (including Freshwater Farm Plans), and community and iwi engagement. Ian, as one of the original creators of farm environment plans has contributed to their development as a tool for farmers and rural communities to see improved environmental outcomes over several decades.
